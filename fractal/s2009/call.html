Call for Abstracts

We invite abstracts, two pages or less, for consideration for presentation at FRACTAL. As always, elegant as well as preliminary solutions to architectural problems are welcome. We also strongly encourage submissions concerning future expected problems. Such abstracts and presentations need not contain solutions, actual or proposed. Authors may present work they have already published elsewhere or plan to publish in the future. We welcome participation the entire front range area. Please send your abstract (email PDF) to fractalworkshop.2009@gmail.com.

Topics: architecture design, program analysis, program verification, embedded systems, language design, reconfigurable computing, compilers, virtual machines, profiling.
