Synthesis from Linear-Time Specifications

Game theoretic approaches to synthesis of controllers have been explored for over twenty years, but practical applications are only now emerging.

In this talk we review some classic results and recent advances, and show how they may be combined to translate a set of linear time properties (typically given in LTL) into an implementation.

We discuss some preliminary results.

Fabio Somenzi, University of Colorado at Boulder
