Call for Presentations

We invite brief abstracts (no more than 300 words) for consideration for presentation at FRACTAL. As always, elegant as well as preliminary solutions are welcome. We strongly encourage submissions concerning future expected problems. Such abstracts and presentations need not contain solutions, actual or proposed. Please indicate if your presentation has already been given at another venue. Preferential treatment will be given to up-and-coming work that has not yet been presented elsewhere. Please send your title and abstract (email text or PDF) to fractal.workshop@gmail.com.

Topics: architecture design, program analysis, program verification, embedded systems, language design, reconfigurable computing, compilers, virtual machines, profiling, cluster or distributed computing
