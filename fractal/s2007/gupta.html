De-con-struction of the Polyhedral Model

The Polyhedral Model has been extensively used for the analysis and transformation of regular loop programs. In my talk, I'll contrive some examples that explain the motivations for the development of the model. Using the properties of the model, I will elaborate on (i) the correctness of transformations and analyses (in other words, why everything works the way it seems to work), and (ii) the design of an equational language based on the model. Then the talk will present compelling reasons for "de-struction" of the polyhedral model.

Gautam Gupta, Colorado State University
