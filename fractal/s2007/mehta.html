Chip Floorplanning

Floorplanning is a step in VLSI design that determines locations of components on a chip based on criteria that have traditionally included chip area and interconnect length. Floorplanning is increasingly becoming relevant during architectural design because component locations impact interconnect delays, which in turn, impact clock frequency. This talk describes our research in floorplanning and outlines some challenges for the future.

Dinesh Mehta, Colorado School of Mines
