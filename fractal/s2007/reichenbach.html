Guided Refactorings

Refactoring is the process of transforming a program into a structurally different program with the same behaviour. Most modern software development tools include refactoring support, but are inconvenient in one of two ways: Either they perform more transformations than requested, or they refuse some desirable transformations. We present a novel approach to refactoring which suffers from neither limitation.

Christoph Reichenbach, University of Colorado at Boulder
