Algorithmic optimizations

Language semantics often prevent many reasonable optimizations. In this talk, I'll describe an optimization system that performs aggressive optimizations by interacting with the user. Specifically, our system identifies near misses for optimizations and for these near misses, it comes up with reasons why the optimization was inapplicable. The user can enable the optimization by providing assumptions that address the reasons.
