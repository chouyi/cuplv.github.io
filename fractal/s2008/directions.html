Directions to the University of Denver

By car

DU's campus is easily accessible from I-25, the main route running through the Denver metro area north/south. Campus lies just south and west of the intersection of I-25 and University Blvd.

To reach University Blvd.
  • From the east (Denver International Airport):
    1. From the airport, take Pena Blvd. to I-70 West (exit to the left)
    2. Take I-70 to I-25 South (Exit 274)
    3. Take I-25 to University Blvd. (Exit 205)
    4. Turn right on University Blvd.
  • From the west:
    1. Take I-70 to 6th Avenue East (Exit 261)
    2. Take 6th Avenue to I-25 South
    3. Take I-25 to University Blvd. (Exit 205)
    4. Turn right on University Blvd.
  • From the north and south:
    1. Take I-25 to University Blvd. (Exit 205)
    2. Turn south on University Blvd.
From University Blvd.
Campus runs along the right-hand (west) side of University Blvd., between Buchtel Blvd. and Harvard Ave. Sturm Hall is at the SE intersection of Ashbury and Race (right of the green arrow at the bottom left corner in this image ). Visitor parking is at the SW corner of Ashbury and Race. Parking passes will be given out when you register on Oct 12. turn right on Warren Circle. (Warren Circle is also designated Daniel L. Ritchie Memorial Way.)

By light rail

You can reach campus from RTD light rail lines E, F and H (PDF). Exit the train at the University of Denver station, which is at the north end of campus. (See light rail schedules and fares.)

By bus

You can reach campus via three RTD bus routes:

  • Route 21 - Runs east/west from Lakewood to Aurora
  • Route 24 - Runs north/south along University Blvd. from C-470 to 41st Ave.
  • Route 52 - Runs from northwest Denver to southeast Denver
